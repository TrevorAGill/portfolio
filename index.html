About Me

I am a Salesforce.com certified administrator and developer with 4+ years experience in the platform. I believe that the most important asset of any organization is the attention, time, and creativity of the people who work at that organization. I find the most satisfaction in my job in automating repetitive or click-heavy tasks, creating improved access to actionable data, or otherwise streamlining burdensome business processes in order to make co-workers jobs more enjoyable and free up capacity for the kind of creative work and strategizing that that actually requires a human brain.
